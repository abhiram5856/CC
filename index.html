About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a comprehensive student networking platform designed to bridge the gap between students, seniors, and alumni. Our mission is to create a supportive ecosystem where knowledge sharing, mentorship, and collaboration thrive.

Our Vision

To become the go-to platform for students seeking guidance, opportunities, and meaningful connections within their academic community and beyond.

What We Offer

  • Mentorship Hub: Connect with experienced seniors and alumni for personalized guidance
  • Events & Opportunities: Discover workshops, hackathons, internships, and campus events
  • Skill Swap & Team Finder: Learn from peers and find teammates for projects
  • Anonymous Chat: Ask questions and share concerns safely
